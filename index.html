Our Heritage

Based in the heart of Chicago's Loop, Diamond Source was built on a simple belief: trust is earned through transparency. We are not a traditional retail store, we are specialized buyers with deep expertise in diamonds and vintage jewelry. Each piece valued not just for its weight, but for its history and craftsmanship.

Ethical Excellence

We focus exclusively on natural diamonds. No lab-grown, no synthetics, only stones formed by the earth over millions of years, valued for their rarity, integrity and enduring worth.
